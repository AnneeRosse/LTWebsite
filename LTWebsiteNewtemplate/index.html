B2B and B2C Commerce Management

In SAP Commerce Cloud, the backoffice is like a control center. It helps businesses ...handle two main types of sales: B2B (Business-to-Business): This is when one business sells to another. The backoffice helps manage big orders, special prices for different customers, and business accounts. B2C (Business-to-Consumer): This is when a business sells directly to individual shoppers. The backoffice here focuses on making shopping easy for individual customers, like showing personalized products or tracking orders. The SAP Commerce backoffice helps businesses manage both big business sales and individual customer sales in one place.
