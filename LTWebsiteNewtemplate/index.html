Our Ecommerce Development Process


...

At Ladera, we understand the transformative power of SAP Commerce Cloud. Our Center of Excellence (CoE) is dedicated to helping businesses harness the full potential of this e-commerce solution.

Pre-development analysis and planning

We dive deep into your business requirements and develop a detailed development plan with key deliverables, budget, and timeline.

Prototyping

Initially, you will receive prototypes for three main pages: product page, category page, and homepage. After testing and optimizing their UI/UX, we get your approval and move to design your actual store.

Design development

You can always choose a pre-built theme for your store. However, if you want to stand out, we will create a custom design that meets your customers’ expectations and business objectives.

Store development

We construct your ecommerce architecture from the ground up, customize all website features, and import customers and products into your store. The development phase is delivered in sprints.

Extensions and integrations

The store is integrated with extensions and third-party systems of your choice. If you’re not sure which system works best for your business needs, we’ll help you pick and integrate one.

Quality control

We carefully test all the features and integrations, fix bugs, and address tech issues to prevent security breaches and potential data losses.

Project release

Your store is up and running! After a project sign-off, you can keep partnering with us on ecommerce consultation and post-release support and maintenance.
